Client and Servers

Computers connected to the Web are called clients and servers.

  • Clients are the recipient of a good, service, product or an idea - obtained from a seller, vendor, or supplier via a financial transaction or exchange for money or some other valuable consideration.
  • Servers are people whose responsibility it is to provide assistance to another person, or a centralized computer system or program that holds data to which multiple users can connect. A waiter at a restaurant who brings you your meal is an example of a server.
